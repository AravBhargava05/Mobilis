Transforming Lives with Accessibility

At Mobilis, we are driven by a singular mission: to make high-quality, functional prosthetics accessible to the millions of amputees in developing countries who have historically been left behind. With over 40 million amputees worldwide, only a small fraction have access to the prosthetics and healthcare they need. A Prosthetic Designed for Independence

Unlike traditional prosthetics, which require skilled professionals to fit and adjust, the Mobilis Arm is designed for self-fitting. This unique feature allows amputees to use the arm independently, without relying on specialized healthcare services. In areas where medical resources are limited, this design ensures that amputees can receive and use the prosthetic without waiting for appointments or traveling long distances.

Many customizable options to fit each unique limb
